Understanding Global Warming

What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the long-term rise in Earth's average temperature due to human activities, especially the emission of greenhouse gases. It affects ecosystems, sea levels, and weather patterns.

Causes of Global Warming

  • Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, and gas)
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial emissions
  • Agricultural practices
